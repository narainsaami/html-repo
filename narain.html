Narain

Narain, the Guardian of Light, is a hero unlike any other. With the power to harness the energy of the sun, he can create blinding light shields, summon solar blasts, and heal others with radiant energy. Originating from a humble village, Narain dedicated his life to protecting the weak and bringing justice to those who misuse power. Clad in a suit that glows with golden armor and intricate patterns reflecting ancient scripts, Narain stands tall with a calm aura and fierce resolve. His mission is not only to safeguard his homeland but to inspire hope and unity across the world.
